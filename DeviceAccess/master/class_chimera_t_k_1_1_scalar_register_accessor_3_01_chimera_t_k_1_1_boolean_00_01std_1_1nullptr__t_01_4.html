Detailed Description

template<>
class ChimeraTK::ScalarRegisterAccessor< ChimeraTK::Boolean, std::nullptr_t >

Template specialisation for ChimeraTK::Boolean.

Simply add implicit type conversions to references of bool, since C++ does not do this conversion on its own as it involves two steps.
